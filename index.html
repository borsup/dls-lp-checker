DLS/LP Checker

This tool cross-checks the Departure Loading Sheet (DLS) against the Load Plan PDF (LP) for cargo flights.

It verifies three things:

  • ULD number match & its corresponding weight (per LP position)
  • ULDs listed in the DLS but absent from the LP
  • ULDs found in the LP but not present in the DLS

All processing is done locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
